About Dr. Y. D. Sookoo

Dr. Y. D. Sookoo is a highly qualified General Practitioner (MBChB) with a passion for community healthcare and wellness innovation. With years of experience serving the Phoenix community, Dr. Sookoo combines medical expertise with genuine compassion.

Specializing in women's health and preventive care, Dr. Sookoo believes in a holistic approach to medicine that addresses both physical and emotional wellbeing. Her practice integrates traditional medical care with modern wellness treatments like IV therapy to provide comprehensive health solutions.

Committed to making quality healthcare accessible, Dr. Sookoo has built a practice known for its welcoming environment, family-friendly approach, and dedication to patient education.
